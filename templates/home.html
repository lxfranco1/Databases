Twitter Analyzer


How does the Twitter Analyzer Work?

Simply click on the Analyzer tab and input the URL of your desired tweet, then travel to the results page to view the
Twitter Analyzer's findings.

What is the Twitter Analyzer?

Twitter is widely known as a system for users to express public opinion, and can somewhat reflect the general
standing of a topic if analyzed thoroughly. The Twitter Analyzer seeks to analyze this user data and express the exact level of
influence and scope of spread a tweet has attained in ways that remain quite unclear when observed through normal channels


What are it's applications?

A big question regarding information spreading across twitter is just how far the influence of this information has travelled. For example,
many politicians question how far campaign messages travel, and whom they travel to. The twitter analyzer will break down this
information, albeit on a smaller scale, and break down the information, store it to a database, and readily query the information for the users viewing pleasure.
